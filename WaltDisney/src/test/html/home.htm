Did You Know?

  • With profits from Snow White, Walt Disney made a deposit on 51 acres of land in Burbank, CA, which The Walt Disney Studios still occupies today.
  • "Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man's Chest" is the first Disney film to cross the $1 Billion mark at the global box office.
  • In Disney's 2010 hit film Alice in Wonderland the potion Alice drinks to shrink is called "Pishsolver." The cake she eats to grow is called "Upelkuchen."
